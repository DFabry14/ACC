About Aaron's Coffee Corner

Hi! Thanks for checking out Aaron's Coffee Corner! We started on September 3, Labor Day, and as you can see we have reached 63% of our goal. We are so grateful to everyone that has contributed! Let's make it 100%!

Please join our family in making a difference for the families of critically ill patients cared for at Ann & Robert H. Lurie Children’s Hospital of Chicago.

Our family is raising funds through Lurie Children’s Circle of Friends program to support Aaron's Coffee Corner, a program that will provide 24/7 access to free, fresh, quality coffee in the Family Great Room of the Pediatric Intensive Care Unit on the 16th floor.

Join our effort to bring the smallest of comforts to all those that care for sick kids whether they are parents, guardians, caregivers, family or friends.

Proximity is key! No one wants to miss a doctor drop-in for an early morning progress check. Or, maybe there are concerns about the often unpredicatable timing of daily rounds. Or, worst case scenario, your child just isn't doing very well. You can help provide a little peace of mind and a sense of security and comfort by helping us make sure there is access to a fresh cup of coffee just steps away.

They call it a Family Great Room - let’s make sure families and their support team know that we think they are GREAT!

Join us in acknowledging their commitment, tenacity, courage and strength.

With your help we can provide a sense of comfort and familiarity in a far too unfamiliar place.
